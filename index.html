About Me

Hi, I am Sam Stubbings and I am a 3rd Year Gameplay Programmer studying Games Computing at the University of Lincoln.



I am mainly interested in procedural content generation and Virtual Reality,

though I have a keen interest in networking and I am actively trying to explore new areas of game development that interest me.



I also actively play video games as a hobby and I frequently take part in game jams, including the Global Game Jam and Ludum Dare.

I enjoy participating in game jams as they give me the opportunity to push myself and to better my skills both on my own and in a team.
